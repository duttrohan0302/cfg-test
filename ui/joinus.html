Patiala.City

Patiala.city is a community platform made with only one agenda in mind, to give the residents of Patiala and tourists a chance to explore what this city has to offer in it's entirety. This app will be a hub for all services available in Patiala such as hotel information, event calendar and portal, job vacancies whereas it'll also serve as a social media platform made exclusively for the people in the city with it's blogs, podcasts, radio and news being completely city centric.
